Defade is an innovating infrastructure for a minecraft 1.19 mini-game server.

💫 What makes us different?

  • A simple, stable and efficient infrastructure
    • Run on Yokura, our Minestom fork, featuring a home-made pathfinder and more...
    • We've created Amethyst, a compact file format to save minecraft chunks quickly
    • A VPS to host our dependencies and our proxy (Velocity fork)
  • Unique maps
  • Documentation for each mini-game
  • A motivated team to complete the project
